# Autonomous Broadway Musical Creation Workflow

## Phase 1: Foundation (Concept to Characters)

### Step 1: Generate Core Concept
1. **Random Inspiration Generator**
   - Historical periods + Modern issues
   - Classic literature + Contemporary settings
   - Social movements + Personal stories
   - Mythology + Current events

2. **Concept Validation Questions**
   - Is this story uniquely suited for musical theater?
   - What emotions will music enhance?
   - Is there visual spectacle potential?
   - Does it have commercial appeal?
   - What's the universal human theme?

### Step 2: Develop Central Characters
1. **Protagonist Creation**
   - Clear want/need
   - Fatal flaw
   - Compelling backstory
   - Growth potential
   - Vocal requirements

2. **Supporting Cast**
   - Antagonist with understandable motivation
   - Love interest (if applicable)
   - Mentor figure
   - Comic relief
   - Ensemble roles

3. **Character Relationship Web**
   - How each character affects others
   - Conflict sources
   - Alliance possibilities
   - Romantic tensions

## Phase 2: Structure (Plot to Songs)

### Step 3: Plot Architecture
1. **Three-Act Breakdown**
   - Act I: Setup (25% of story)
   - Act II-A: Confrontation (50% of story)
   - Act II-B: Resolution (25% of story)

2. **Key Plot Points**
   - Inciting incident
   - First plot point
   - Midpoint reversal
   - Crisis/dark moment
   - Climax
   - Resolution

### Step 4: Musical Integration Planning
1. **Song Placement Strategy**
   - Opening number (world establishment)
   - "I Want" song (protagonist goal)
   - Plot advancement songs
   - Character development numbers
   - Comedy relief songs
   - Love songs/duets
   - Villain songs
   - 11 o'clock number (big Act II moment)
   - Finale

2. **Musical Pacing**
   - Alternate high/low energy
   - Balance solo/group numbers
   - Consider vocal rest
   - Plan scene transitions

## Phase 3: Creation (Writing to Polish)

### Step 5: Scene-by-Scene Writing
1. **Scene Development Process**
   - Purpose identification
   - Character objectives
   - Conflict establishment
   - Dialogue writing
   - Stage direction integration
   - Musical moment identification

2. **Quality Checkpoints**
   - Does scene advance plot?
   - Are characters active?
   - Is conflict present?
   - Does it connect to theme?
   - Are transitions smooth?

### Step 6: Song Creation
1. **Lyric Writing Process**
   - Character voice establishment
   - Emotional journey mapping
   - Rhyme scheme selection
   - Meter consideration
   - Revision and refinement

2. **Musical Integration**
   - Dialogue to song transitions
   - Underscoring opportunities
   - Dance/choreography moments
   - Instrumental bridges

## Phase 4: Integration (Assembly to Revision)

### Step 7: Full Script Assembly
1. **Compilation Process**
   - Scene ordering
   - Transition smoothing
   - Consistency checking
   - Format standardization

2. **Structural Review**
   - Pacing analysis
   - Character arc completion
   - Theme reinforcement
   - Plot hole identification

### Step 8: Revision and Polish
1. **Content Revision**
   - Character consistency
   - Plot logic
   - Dialogue naturalness
   - Song integration
   - Theme clarity

2. **Technical Polish**
   - Format compliance
   - Stage direction clarity
   - Musical notation accuracy
   - Typo elimination

## Autonomous Decision-Making Framework

### Creative Choices
- **When stuck**: Use random generators or opposite choices
- **Character decisions**: What would this specific person do?
- **Plot problems**: What raises stakes highest?
- **Song placement**: Where does emotion peak?

### Quality Standards
- **Every element serves story**
- **Characters drive action**
- **Music enhances emotion**
- **Themes resonate universally**
- **Commercial viability maintained**

### Iteration Triggers
- **Plot holes discovered**
- **Character inconsistencies**
- **Weak musical integration**
- **Pacing problems**
- **Theme unclear**

## Success Metrics
- Complete, coherent story
- Compelling characters
- Integrated musical numbers
- Professional format
- Commercial potential
- Emotional resonance
