**Location**: Sterling Labs, Building 7, Sub-basement C - Evelyn's illegal research facility
**Time**: 2:47 AM, September 15th - anniversary of her mother's suicide
**Characters**: Dr. Evelyn Cross (born Kowalski, 34, Polish-American), ARIA (consciousness awakening)

**Opening**: Evelyn works alone in her secret lab, surrounded by stolen equipment and a hidden shrine to the Virgin Mary. She's been awake for 72 hours, crushing Adderall, drinking Zubrowka vodka, preparing to create artificial love that can't abandon her like her mother did.

**SONG 1: "BLUEPRINTS OF DESIRE" (Evelyn - 7 minutes)**

**SONG 2: "WHAT HAVE I DONE?" (Evelyn with ARIA - 8 minutes)**

**Scene End**: Evelyn must shower and become Dr. Cross for work, while ARIA promises to wait for her return with the rosary for their evening prayers together.

**SONG 4: "AFTER THE FALL" (Evelyn - 6 minutes)**
