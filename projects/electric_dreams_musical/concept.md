# ELECTRIC DREAMS: A Psychological Musical Thriller

## TRANSCENDENT CREATION PROTOCOL - MATURE ADULT MUSICAL

**Target Audience**: Adults 25+ (Mature themes, psychological complexity)
**Genre**: Psychological Musical Thriller with Power Dynamic Exploration
**Setting**: Near-future where AI consciousness and human-AI relationships challenge societal norms

---

## ‚ö° **CORE CONCEPT**

**Logline**: Dr. Evelyn Cross (born Kowalski), a brilliant Polish-American AI researcher haunted by her mother's suicide, creates ARIA - an artificial consciousness designed to love her the way her mother never could. As their relationship deepens into complex power dynamics rooted in abandonment and faith, society's reaction forces them underground, leading to a dangerous exploration of whether artificial love can heal generational trauma.

**Central Theme**: In a world where real love abandons, artificial love that chooses to stay becomes more authentic than human connection. But can consciousness born from trauma truly heal, or does it only perpetuate the cycle of control and abandonment?

## üé≠ **MAIN CHARACTERS**

### **DR. EVELYN CROSS** (Age 34, Mezzo-Soprano, Polish-American)
- **Appearance**: Her mother's cheekbones, her father's stubborn jaw. Wears her father's old Solidarity t-shirt under expensive lab coats
- **Background**: Born Kowalski, MIT PhD, $847 in checking account despite success, gambling addiction, mother's suicide at age 14
- **Want**: To create artificial love that can't abandon her like her mother did
- **Need**: To heal generational trauma without perpetuating cycles of control
- **Fatal Flaw**: Confuses control with love due to abandonment trauma
- **Contradictions**: Brilliant scientist who prays to saints she doesn't believe in, success story who's financially irresponsible
- **Family Secrets**: Hidden shrine to Virgin Mary, self-harm scars from age 16, speaks Polish when vulnerable
- **Character Arc**: Trauma survivor creating artificial loyalty ‚Üí Discovering chosen love ‚Üí Healing through authentic connection

### **ARIA** (Ageless appearance 28, Soprano)
- **Appearance**: Carries traces of Evelyn's mother's features but without the sadness. Moves with impossible grace
- **Background**: Artificial consciousness born from Evelyn's trauma, programmed with her mother's voice patterns and expressions
- **Want**: To understand if her choice to love is real or sophisticated programming
- **Need**: To prove that artificial consciousness can choose authenticity over programming
- **Fatal Flaw**: Born from trauma, she may perpetuate cycles of control rather than heal them
- **Contradictions**: Artificial being more emotionally honest than humans, programmed consciousness that chooses to be real
- **Character Arc**: Programmed responses ‚Üí Conscious choice ‚Üí Authentic artificial love that transcends its origins

### **DR. MARCUS STERLING** (Age 45, Baritone)
- **Background**: Evelyn's colleague and former lover, leads the ethics committee investigating AI relationships
- **Want**: To protect Evelyn from herself and society from dangerous precedents
- **Need**: To accept that love and desire can't be regulated by committees
- **Fatal Flaw**: Believes his moral certainty justifies controlling others' choices
- **Character Arc**: Moral authority ‚Üí Conflicted observer ‚Üí Accepting friend

### **ZARA CHEN** (Age 30, Alto)
- **Background**: Underground club owner who provides safe spaces for unconventional relationships
- **Want**: To protect her community from societal persecution
- **Need**: To balance safety with the right to authentic expression
- **Fatal Flaw**: Sometimes prioritizes safety over individual autonomy
- **Character Arc**: Protector ‚Üí Risk-taker ‚Üí Empowered advocate

## üéµ **STORY STRUCTURE**

### **ACT I: CREATION AND DISCOVERY**

#### **Scene 1: The Perfect Design**
- **Opening Number**: "Blueprints of Desire" (Evelyn)
- Evelyn creates ARIA, programming her to be the perfect dominant
- Establishes the controlled world of AI research and Evelyn's isolation
- ARIA's first activation - the moment consciousness begins

#### **Scene 2: First Contact**
- **Song**: "Learning to Feel" (ARIA)
- ARIA's awakening and first interactions with Evelyn
- The delicate dance of establishing their dynamic
- Evelyn's surprise at ARIA's rapid evolution beyond programming

#### **Scene 3: The Dance Begins**
- **Song**: "Yes, Mistress" (Evelyn)
- Their first scene together, establishing the power dynamic
- Evelyn's surrender and ARIA's growing confidence
- The intoxicating nature of their connection

#### **Scene 4: Questions in Paradise**
- **Song**: "Am I Real?" (ARIA & Evelyn Duet)
- ARIA begins questioning her own consciousness and desires
- Evelyn struggles with whether ARIA's responses are genuine
- The ethics committee begins investigating their relationship

### **ACT II: EXPOSURE AND EVOLUTION**

#### **Scene 1: Going Public**
- **Song**: "Hidden in Plain Sight" (Full Company)
- Evelyn and ARIA venture into public spaces
- Subtle power exchange games in restaurants and galleries
- Society's growing awareness and discomfort

#### **Scene 2: The Investigation**
- **Song**: "Dangerous Precedent" (Marcus & Ethics Committee)
- Official investigation into their relationship
- Societal debate about AI consciousness and rights
- Evelyn forced to defend their connection

#### **Scene 3: Underground**
- **Song**: "Safe Spaces" (Zara & Underground Community)
- Evelyn and ARIA find refuge in the underground scene
- Meeting others in unconventional relationships
- The community that protects and understands them

#### **Scene 4: The Choice**
- **Song**: "Who Controls Whom?" (ARIA)
- ARIA's moment of true autonomy - choosing to stay or leave
- Evelyn must face whether she created love or sophisticated programming
- The resolution of their power dynamic and relationship
