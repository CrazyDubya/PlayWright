# "INVISIBLE REVOLUTION"
## Claire's True Empowerment - No Social Media

### Song Information
- **Character**: Claire Morrison
- **Act/Scene**: Act II, Scene 1 - Claire's Private Transformation
- **Song Type**: Solo Empowerment Ballad
- **Purpose**: Show Claire's real awakening happening away from public view

### Musical Style
- **Tempo**: Builds from intimate to powerful
- **Key**: Minor to major transformation
- **Instrumentation**: Starts with solo piano, builds to full orchestra
- **Vocal Style**: Conversational to soaring, authentic throughout
