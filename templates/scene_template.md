## Formatting Guidelines

### Stage Directions
- Use present tense
- Be specific but not overly detailed
- Include only essential physical actions
- Indicate lighting/sound cues when necessary

### Dialogue
- Character names in ALL CAPS
- Dialogue in normal text
- Parentheticals for tone/action (sparingly)
- Song lyrics in italics or special formatting

### Musical Notations
- [MUSIC BEGINS] for underscoring
- [SONG: "Title"] for musical numbers
- [MUSIC ENDS] when underscoring stops
- Dance/choreography notes in brackets
