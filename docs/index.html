Our Musicals

Echo: Digital Immortality

Sci-Fi Musical Drama

When neuroscientist Maya Chen discovers her deceased grandmother's consciousness preserved in a tech company's servers, she must navigate the ethics of digital immortality while learning that love and memory can exist beyond death itself.

Electric Dreams

Psychological Musical Thriller

Dr. Evelyn Cross creates ARIA, an artificial consciousness designed to love her unconditionally. As their relationship deepens, society's reaction forces them underground, exploring whether artificial love can heal generational trauma.

Fractured Mirrors

Psychological Musical Thriller

When Dr. Sarah Chen's experimental memory therapy begins erasing the boundaries between her patients' traumas and her own suppressed past, she must navigate a labyrinth of fractured identities to discover which memories are worth saving.

Midnight at the Majestic

Murder Mystery Musical

When Broadway's biggest star is murdered during opening night of her comeback show, the entire cast becomes suspects in a deadly game where everyone has secrets, everyone has motives, and the show must go on.

Neon Hearts

Burlesque Musical Drama

When the last independent burlesque theater in 1960s Las Vegas faces closure, a washed-up headliner and a naive newcomer must choose between saving the club that made them or pursuing the dreams that might destroy them.

Neon Rebellion

Teen Rock Musical

In a dystopian future where all music is AI-generated and human creativity is illegal, 17-year-old Zara discovers an underground movement of teen rebels who risk everything to create authentic art.

Picket Fence Prison

Suburban Drama Musical

A suburban housewife's journey from empty-nest numbness to authentic self-discovery through dangerous power games that force her to choose between safety and truth.

Rainbow Academy

Children's Musical Mystery

When the magical Rainbow Crystal protecting their kingdom goes missing, Princess Luna and her anxious unicorn Stardust must overcome their fears and lead their classmates in solving Rainbow Academy's greatest mystery.

The Silly Magic Academy

Children's Comedy Musical

A delightful children's musical about young wizards at a magical academy where spells go hilariously wrong, teaching valuable lessons about friendship, creativity, and embracing mistakes.

Second Act

Contemporary Musical Drama

When a 50-year-old former Broadway dancer gets one last chance to audition for a major revival, she must overcome decades of regret and self-doubt to prove that talent has no expiration date.
