Synopsis

In a world where AI generates all music and human creativity is illegal, 17-year-old Zara discovers an underground movement of teen rebels who risk everything to create authentic art.

Neon Rebellion is a high-energy rock musical set in a dystopian future where all art is generated by corporate algorithms and human creativity is criminalized.
