Synopsis

When the last independent burlesque theater in 1960s Las Vegas faces closure, a washed-up headliner and naive newcomer must choose between saving the club or pursuing dreams that might destroy them.

Set against the neon-soaked backdrop of 1960s Las Vegas, Neon Hearts tells the story of The Velvet Room, the last independent burlesque club on the Strip. Ruby Valentine, a faded headliner, mentors Dolly Monroe, a young performer chasing stardom. As corporate casinos threaten to demolish their home, the company must decide whether to fight for their chosen family or pursue individual dreams.

Principal Characters

Ruby Valentine

Age 35, Lead Female

Headliner at The Velvet Room for 15 years. Real name Rosa Valentini, Italian-American from South Philly.

Character Arc: From refusing to let go to accepting that some dreams must end.

Dolly Monroe
