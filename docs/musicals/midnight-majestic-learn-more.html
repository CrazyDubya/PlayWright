Principal Characters

Vivian Sterling

Age 45, The Victim

Broadway legend making a comeback after mysterious 5-year absence. Was blackmailing half the company.

Character Arc: The victim whose secrets drive the entire mystery.

\n\n

Detective Frank Morrison

Age 42, Baritone

Hardboiled detective who must navigate the theatrical world to find the killer.

Character Arc: From cynical investigator to someone who understands theatrical passion.

\n\n

Katherine 'Kitty' Hartwell

Age 28, Soprano

Rising star who was Vivian's understudy. Stands to inherit the role.

Character Arc: Ambitious actress with secrets about her past.

\n\n

James Brighton

Age 35, Tenor

Leading man with a complicated romantic history with Vivian.

Character Arc: Charming actor hiding financial desperation.

\n\n

Ruby Washington

Age 50, Alto

Stage manager who knows everyone's secrets and keeps the show running.

Character Arc: The one person who sees everything backstage.
