Synopsis

When Broadway's biggest star is murdered during opening night of her comeback show, the entire cast becomes suspects in a deadly game where everyone has secrets, motives, and the show must go on.

Set during the Golden Age of Broadway, Midnight at the Majestic combines classic murder mystery with theatrical glamour. When legendary actress Vivian Sterling is found poisoned in her dressing room during intermission, Detective Frank Morrison must interrogate a cast of suspects‚Äîeach with secrets to hide and motives to kill. As the investigation unfolds, the musical explores themes of ambition, blackmail, and the masks we wear both on and off stage.
