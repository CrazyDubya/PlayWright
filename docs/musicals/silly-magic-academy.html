Synopsis

Young wizards at a magical academy learn valuable lessons about friendship, creativity, and embracing mistakes when their spells create hilarious chaos.

The Silly Magic Academy is a delightful comedy musical perfect for young audiences. At a magical school where everything goes hilariously wrong, young wizards learn that mistakes are opportunities to learn, creativity is more important than perfection, and friendship makes everything better. Through silly spells, talking animals, and magical mishaps, students discover that laughter and learning go hand in hand.
