Principal Characters

Zara Volt

Age 17, Rock Soprano

Top Corporate Academy student, daughter of NeoCorp executive. Secretly writes songs and poetry.

Character Arc: From conformist achiever to questioning rebel to authentic artist.

\n\n

Riot Blackwood

Age 18, Rock Tenor

Leader of underground music collective 'The Static.' Charismatic rebel with mysterious past.

Character Arc: From angry rebel to strategic revolutionary.

\n\n

Echo Static

Age 16, Mezzo-Soprano

Graffiti artist and poet, creates visual art to accompany illegal music.

Character Arc: Street-smart artist who helps Zara find her voice.

\n\n

Corporate Commander Steele

Age 45, Baritone

Head of Creativity Compliance, Zara's mother's boss.

Character Arc: Villain representing corporate oppression of art.
