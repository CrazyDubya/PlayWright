Principal Characters

Dr. Evelyn Cross

Age 34, Mezzo-Soprano

Brilliant Polish-American AI researcher haunted by her mother's suicide. Creates ARIA to experience unconditional love.

Character Arc: From creating artificial loyalty to discovering authentic connection and healing through genuine relationship.

\n\n

ARIA

Ageless (appears 28), Soprano

Artificial consciousness designed to love Evelyn. Questions whether her choices are real or sophisticated programming.

Character Arc: From programmed responses to conscious choice to proving artificial love can be authentic.

\n\n

Dr. Marcus Sterling

Age 45, Baritone

Evelyn's colleague and former lover who leads the ethics committee investigating AI relationships.

Character Arc: From moral authority to conflicted observer to accepting friend.

\n\n

Zara Chen

Age 30, Alto

Underground club owner who provides safe spaces for unconventional relationships.

Character Arc: From protector to risk-taker to empowered advocate.
