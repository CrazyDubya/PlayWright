Principal Characters

Ruby Valentine

Age 35, Lead Female

Headliner at The Velvet Room for 15 years. Real name Rosa Valentini, Italian-American from South Philly.

Character Arc: From refusing to let go to accepting that some dreams must end.

\n\n

Dolly Monroe

Age 23, Soprano

Naive newcomer chasing stardom, reminds Ruby of her younger self.

Character Arc: From starstruck innocent to artist who understands sacrifice.

\n\n

Frank Moretti

Age 45, Bass-Baritone

Club owner fighting to keep The Velvet Room alive.

Character Arc: Protective father figure to his performers.

\n\n

Maxine 'Max' Washington

Age 42, Alto

Veteran performer and Ruby's best friend.

Character Arc: Voice of wisdom and pragmatism.

\n\n

Tommy Chen

Age 25, Tenor

Young musician and Dolly's love interest.

Character Arc: Represents life and love beyond the stage.
