Synopsis

A suburban housewife's journey from empty-nest numbness to authentic self-discovery through dangerous power games that force her to choose between safety and truth.

Picket Fence Prison explores the dark underbelly of suburban perfection. Claire Morrison, experiencing empty-nest syndrome, begins a dangerous relationship with David, a mysterious delivery driver. As their power games intensify, Claire must choose between the safety of her comfortable life and the dangerous truth of authentic self-discovery. The musical examines themes of suburban imprisonment, desire, risk, and the cost of living authentically.
