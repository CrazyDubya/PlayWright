Principal Characters

Dr. Sarah Chen

Age 35, Mezzo-Soprano

Neuroscientist specializing in trauma-based memory disorders. Uses patients' healing to avoid her own suppressed trauma.

Character Arc: From healer hiding wounds to integrated survivor to authentic therapist.

\n\n

Marcus Torres

Age 28, Tenor

Combat veteran with severe PTSD and memory fragmentation.

Character Arc: From fragmented soldier to integrated person finding peace.

\n\n

Lily Park

Age 22, Soprano

Young woman with dissociative identity disorder from childhood abuse.

Character Arc: From fractured identities to choosing which parts of herself to keep.

\n\n

Dr. Elizabeth Monroe

Age 55, Alto

Sarah's mentor who recognizes Sarah's avoidance patterns.

Character Arc: Wise guide helping Sarah confront her past.
