# Zara Okafor - Character Profile

## Basic Information
- **Name**: Zara Okafor
- **Age**: 26
- **Occupation**: Digital rights activist, freelance journalist, Maya's best friend
- **Social Status**: Grassroots organizer, social media influencer for justice causes
- **Location**: San Francisco, lives in shared activist house

## Physical Description
- **Appearance**: 5'7", athletic build, natural hair in protective styles, vibrant clothing
- **Voice Type**: Soprano (powerful, clear, with great range and belt capability)
- **Movement Style**: Dynamic, expressive, comfortable commanding crowds
- **Distinctive Features**: Multiple ear piercings, activist buttons, always carries protest signs

## Personality
- **Core Traits**: Passionate, fearless, loyal, strategic, sometimes impatient
- **Strengths**: Natural leader, inspiring speaker, tech-savvy, builds coalitions
- **Flaws/Weaknesses**: Sometimes prioritizes cause over people, can be self-righteous
- **Fears**: Injustice going unchallenged, losing friends to the fight, being ineffective
- **Desires/Goals**: Digital justice, corporate accountability, protecting vulnerable people
- **Motivations**: Family history of exploitation, belief in collective action

## Background
- **Family History**: Nigerian immigrant parents, father exploited by tech company
- **Education**: UC Berkeley (Computer Science + Political Science), dropped out to organize
- **Past Experiences**: Led successful campaigns against data privacy violations
- **Formative Events**: Father's wrongful termination by tech company, police surveillance of family
- **Secrets**: Sometimes doubts whether activism makes real difference

## Relationships
- **Family**: Close to parents despite their worry about her activism
- **Friends**: Maya (best friend since college), wide network of activists
- **Romantic Interests**: Recently single, ended relationship over partner's corporate job
- **Enemies/Rivals**: Corporate executives, surveillance state, digital exploitation
- **Mentors**: Veteran civil rights activists, digital privacy pioneers

## Character Arc
- **Starting Point**: Angry activist focused on fighting systems
- **Journey**: Learns to balance activism with personal relationships and strategic thinking
- **Ending Point**: Strategic advocate who builds lasting change through coalition
- **Lessons Learned**: Sustainable change requires both passion and patience
