## Song Information
- **Title**: Magic Goes Wrong (And That's Alright!)

- **Tempo**: Fast, bouncy, circus-like

## Performance Notes

### Staging & Choreography
- **Formation**: Students in a line, each gets spotlight for their magic
- **Movement**: Exaggerated reactions to each magical mishap
- **Props**: Wands, spell components, things to transform
- **Effects**: Safe stage magic for each "wrong" spell

### Individual Character Moments
- **Boom**: Dramatic wand wave, surprised by explosion, proud of rainbow
- **Flutter**: Confident takeoff, confused backward flight, graceful acceptance
- **Muddle**: Careful potion mixing, dismay at color, shrug and smile
- **Tickle**: Serious cleaning attempt, giggles at own spell, spreads joy
- **Snooze**: Sleepy spell casting, gentle snoring, flowers singing lullaby

### Sound Effects & Music
- **Live sound effects**: Cast members making magical sounds
- **Musical stings**: Each mishap gets its own musical punctuation
- **Building energy**: Each verse gets slightly faster and more chaotic
- **Audience cues**: Moments for audience to gasp, laugh, cheer

### Comedy Elements
- **Physical comedy**: Exaggerated reactions, silly movements
- **Verbal comedy**: Rhyming mishaps, unexpected outcomes
- **Visual comedy**: Colorful effects, costume changes, props
- **Interactive comedy**: Audience participation in sound effects

### Educational Value
- **Acceptance**: Everyone's different, and that's wonderful
- **Resilience**: When things go wrong, try again
- **Joy**: Finding happiness in unexpected outcomes
- **Community**: Supporting each other through mistakes
- **Creativity**: Thinking outside the box

### Technical Requirements
- **Lighting**: Quick changes for each character's moment
- **Sound**: Multiple sound effect cues, musical underscoring
- **Props**: Transformable items, wands, spell components
- **Costumes**: Quick reveals, magical accessories
- **Safety**: All effects must be child-safe

### Audience Engagement
- **Call and response**: "Magic goes wrong!" "And that's alright!"
- **Sound effects**: Kids make magical sounds with cast
- **Movement**: Simple gestures kids can copy
- **Participation**: Cheering for each character's attempt

This song establishes that the academy celebrates uniqueness and that "failure" is just another way to succeed. The fast pace, silly lyrics, and physical comedy will have kids laughing while learning valuable lessons about self-acceptance and perseverance.
