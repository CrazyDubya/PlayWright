# "WE ARE THE MAGIC"
**Company Finale**

## Song Information
- **Title**: We Are the Magic
- **Character(s)**: Full Company, Audience Participation
- **Act/Scene**: Act 2, Finale
- **Song Type**: Company Finale/Celebration
- **Purpose**: Celebrate growth, reinforce themes, send audience home happy

## Performance Notes

### Staging Spectacular
- **Full stage**: Every character, every level, maximum spectacle
- **Audience inclusion**: Encourage standing, clapping, singing
- **Movement**: Big, joyful choreography everyone can follow
- **Props**: Wands, sparkles, magical effects for everyone
- **Costumes**: Full magical regalia, bright and colorful

### Character Showcase
- **Every character**: Gets a moment to shine and show growth
- **Pip**: Confident leader, welcoming others
- **Perfecta**: Embraces imperfection with joy
- **Whisper**: Strong, clear voice
- **Professor**: Proud mentor watching students succeed
- **Sparkles**: Fabulous finale star

### Audience Engagement
- **Sing-along**: Simple, repetitive chorus easy to learn
- **Movement**: Clapping, stomping, waving - full participation
- **Call and response**: Multiple opportunities for audience involvement
- **Standing ovation**: Built into the song structure
- **Take-home message**: Clear, positive, memorable

### Technical Finale
- **Lighting**: Full spectrum, magical effects, spotlights
- **Sound**: Full orchestra, maximum energy
- **Effects**: Confetti, bubbles, safe sparklers, disco ball
- **Costumes**: Quick reveals, magical accessories
- **Set**: Everything magical happening at once

### Educational Reinforcement
- **Self-acceptance**: "Different and special in our own way"
- **Community**: "We are the magic" - collective strength
- **Growth**: Each character shows how they've changed
- **Inclusion**: Audience becomes part of the magic
- **Positivity**: Uplifting message to take home

### Musical Elements
- **Key**: A Major - brightest, most triumphant key
- **Rhythm**: Strong 4/4 beat, easy to clap along
- **Melody**: Simple, soaring, memorable
- **Harmonies**: Rich, full company sound
- **Dynamics**: Building from warm to explosive

### Take-Home Message
- **You are magical exactly as you are**
- **Differences make the world more beautiful**
- **Friendship is the greatest magic**
- **Mistakes are opportunities to learn**
- **Everyone belongs somewhere**

This finale sends kids home feeling empowered, accepted, and magical. The participatory elements ensure they feel like part of the show, while the clear message reinforces the musical's core values of self-acceptance and friendship.
